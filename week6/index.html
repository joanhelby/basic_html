Eevee is a Pokémon species in Nintendo and Game Freak's Pokémon franchise. Created by Ken Sugimori, it first appeared in the video games Pokémon Red and Blue. It has later appeared in various merchandise, spinoff titles, and animated and printed adaptations of the franchise. Eevee is a Normal-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It evolves into one of eight different Pokémon through various methods.
